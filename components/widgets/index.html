Components

Billboard

A “billboard” is a styled box used as an emphasized callout, a related sidebar, or otherwise distinctive block of content. The actual contents of a billboard vary; this is just a generic style for a special box.

Look at me!

I live in a box. I live on a one way street.

HTML


<aside class="billboard">
<h2>Look at me!</h2>
<p>I live in a box. I live on a one way street.</p>
</aside>
